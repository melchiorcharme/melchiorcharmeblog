To rub salt in the wound

20 Dec 2025

This entry is a promise to stop drifting and start designing the life I actually want to live.

Recently, it has become obvious that “being productive” is not about squeezing more tasks into the day. It is about choosing fewer, more honest priorities and showing up for them consistently.

The intent behind this blog is simple: write in public to keep myself accountable, notice what truly matters to me, and deliberately make room for it in my schedule.

Over time these posts should become a record of experiments in focus, discipline, and building a life that feels self-directed rather than reactive.
